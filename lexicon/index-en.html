SemanticOps Lexicon v1.0

Open standard for meaning-operated systems and organizations

Welcome to the official repository of the SemanticOps Lexicon v1.0, the first public and governed version of the foundational concepts that structure the SemanticOps operational framework.

This document defines a formal, versioned and traceable vocabulary that enables governing meaning across architectures, processes, models and AI agents aligned with this paradigm.

0. Purpose of the Lexicon

The Lexicon establishes operational definitions that align concepts, processes, models and agents around a common, governed vocabulary, reducing semantic ambiguity across organizational and technical ecosystems.

3. Versioning Structure

The Lexicon adopts an explicit versioning scheme (major.minor.patch) enabling controlled updates while preserving compatibility with models, pipelines or graphs depending on previous definitions.

Governance defines the roles, review workflows and acceptance criteria ensuring internal consistency and alignment with the foundational principles of SemanticOps.

The Lexicon is an open standard. The community may propose new terms, semantic revisions or drift reports through the formal contribution flows provided in the repository (e.g., GitHub issue templates).
